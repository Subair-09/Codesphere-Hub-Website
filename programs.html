Our Programs

Comprehensive initiatives to build the next generation of tech talent

Codesphere Hub Study Groups

Collaborative learning environments where participants work together to master technical concepts, prepare for certifications, and build projects. Our study groups provide structured learning paths with peer support and mentorship.

Join a Study Group

Codesphere Hub Hackathon

Regular innovation challenges that bring together developers, designers, and problem-solvers to create solutions for real-world problems. Participants gain hands-on experience, network with industry professionals, and compete for prizes.

Participate in Hackathon

Codesphere Hub Cloud Mentorship

One-on-one guidance from industry experts in cloud technologies including AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud. Our mentorship program helps participants gain practical skills, prepare for certifications, and navigate cloud career paths.

Apply for Mentorship

Codesphere Hub Apprenticeship

Hands-on work experience with our partner companies. Apprentices work on real projects, receive mentorship, and build their professional network while developing the skills needed to launch their tech careers.

Explore Apprenticeships

Codesphere Hub Laptop Sharing Initiative

We provide access to technology for participants who lack the necessary equipment. Through our laptop sharing program, we ensure that financial constraints don't prevent talented individuals from pursuing tech education.

Request a Laptop

Future STEM Stars

In partnership with Katwig & Dale, we visit secondary schools to talk to young girls about careers in STEM. We have a pool of volunteers that mentor these students and work with schools to provide necessary support.

Support STEM Education
