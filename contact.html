Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with NexaScale?

We have various volunteering opportunities including mentoring, workshop facilitation, career coaching, and event support. Fill out our volunteer application form or contact us directly to discuss how you can contribute.

What types of donations do you accept?

We accept financial donations through our website, as well as equipment donations (laptops, tech accessories). We also welcome corporate sponsorships and in-kind donations of services or software licenses.

How can I participate in your programs?

Visit our Programs page to learn about our various initiatives. Each program has specific application requirements and timelines. You can also contact us directly for more information about upcoming opportunities.

Do you offer programs for beginners?

Yes! Many of our programs are designed for people with little to no prior tech experience. Our study groups and mentorship programs often have beginner tracks to help you build foundational skills.
